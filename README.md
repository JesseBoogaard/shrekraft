## ***SHREKRAFT 1.12.2***

### **ShreKraft adds many things to Minecraft**

  1. Shrek as a mob
  2. Farquaad as a mob
  3. Cotton swabs
  3. Earwax
  4. Compacted earwax
  5. Earwax candle
  6. Onions for consumption and trading
  7. Sludge for bathing
  8. Shrek's hut in swamp biomes
  9. Peat as fuel
  10. Iconic tracks from the movie(s) on music discs
